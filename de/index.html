MultiDoge

MultiDoge is a "light" wallet. It syncs with the blockchain by "skimming" through the blockchain, providing fast sync times.

  • https://multidoge.org/?dl=win
  • https://multidoge.org/?dl=lin
  • https://multidoge.org/?dl=osx
Dogecoin Core

Dogecoin Core, on the other hand, is a "full" wallet. It syncs by downloading it, providing a solid-working Dogecoin wallet.

  • dogecoin-1.14.5-win32-setup-unsigned.exe
  • dogecoin-1.14.5-win64-setup-unsigned.exe
  • dogecoin-1.14.5-i686-pc-linux-gnu.tar.gz
  • dogecoin-1.14.5-x86_64-linux-gnu.tar.gz
  • dogecoin-1.14.5-osx.dmg
