Hosted by the Dogecoin Foundation

The Dogecoin Foundation welcomes all Dogecoin developers to the second Dogecoin Hackathon: Dogeathon! Individuals and teams can participate from anywhere online or join key Dogecoin devs in person at a dedicated location in Ericeira, Portugal.

Location

Vila Galé Hotel in Ericeira

Largo dos Navegantes 1, 2655-320 Ericeira, Portugal

For one legendary week, Vila Galé Ericeira becomes the ultimate Doge dev haven. Much hack. Very surf. Wow.Set on the cliffs of Europe's first World Surfing Reserve, this ocean-view venue brings good WiFi, great vibes, and plenty of space to code, collab, and chill. 3 pools (one for tiny humans), comfy rooms, and sea breezes included.

Refuel with fresh fish, sip wine, and watch the sunset while debugging. Or hit the spa for that post-hack sauna and massage. Much recharge. Just 49km from Lisbon, 22km from Sintra. Close to the action, but far enough for focus. One venue. Infinite possibilities. Dogeathon starts here. 🚀💻🔥
